About Me

Bridging AI & Biomedicine

Dedicated researcher and academician pushing the boundaries of computational intelligence

Transforming Healthcare Through Artificial Intelligence

I am a dedicated researcher and academician specializing in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Graph Neural Networks, and Drug Discovery. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun.

My doctoral research at IIT Patna focused on deep generative models for drug discovery and medical image synthesis. I have also worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Louisiana State University, USA, contributing to cutting-edge projects on AI-driven drug discovery and environmental antibiotic resistance prediction.

My research aims to bridge the gap between computational modeling and real-world biomedical applications, ensuring AI-driven solutions for healthcare challenges.

Expertise

Research Interests

Exploring the frontiers of AI in biomedical sciences

Deep Generative Models

Developing GANs, VAEs, and diffusion models for drug molecule generation and medical image synthesis, enabling novel therapeutic discoveries.

Graph Neural Networks

Leveraging GNN architectures for molecular property prediction, drug-target interaction modeling, and protein structure analysis.

AI for Drug Discovery

Applying reinforcement learning and knowledge graphs to accelerate drug discovery pipelines and predict binding affinities.

Antibiotic Resistance

Predicting antibiotic resistance genes using transformer-based models and pretrained protein language models for environmental monitoring.

Medical Image Analysis

Developing deep learning solutions for medical image synthesis, classification, and segmentation using CT, MRI, and histopathology data.

Reinforcement Learning

Implementing RL-based approaches for molecule generation toward target proteins and optimizing molecular structures.
